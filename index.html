About

At SinY we are united by a deep love for acrobatics. We are nerds of acrobatic technique and experts for Inversion.

Combining the knowledge of acrobatic gymnastics with yogic traditions for the general population, into a form of training accessible for everyone. Since 2017 we provide a plethora of freely available information on our social platforms as an open source knowledge base, which had an influence both on acro and the east-european yoga community as a whole.

We also offer Online Training, starting from basic Inversions to high-level handbalancing, by which we sustain our small team of professional acrobats and coaches.

In form of our textbook we are in the process of constructing a comprehensive knowledge base about all the little details of handstands.

You can support us with a subscription or a one-time donation on Patreon.

Our Team

Founded by Galina Nikolaevna Sinyavskaya and Olga Aleksandrovna Yatsenko, Siny Inversion Yoga (short SinY) combines Galinas experience as an acrobatic gymnastics coach with Olgas background in Yoga. Being a small circle that originated from within the acrobatics community of Kyiv, Ukraine, we teach acrobatics and handstands on a high technical level.

Galina Nikolaevna trains her team of acrobats and a small senior group of professional handbalancers and enthusiasts online in Russian, while Olga is the driving force and face of the organisation. Alongside Ivan Labunets and Pavlov Kucherov she is teaching the Online Groups in Ukrainian, Russian and English, Florian in English and German.

Olga is also the main author and editor of both our vlog on Instagram as well as ourSinY Textbook in Russian.

Online Training

Our online training is focused on Handbalancing. We do not sell courses. The training is held live in form of actual online classes, where you can always get feedback from a coach.

We have different groups structured in skill levels and train everyone, from absolute beginners with no training experience whatsoever over those, who have a training background but have not yet developed a stable handstand or are lacking in technique, to advanced practitioners who already can stand on their hands comfortably and want to bring their basic handstand to the next level.

The training is held in Russian or Ukrainian and English. More English and German classes are planned to be available in the future.

Apart from the handbalancing focused groups with fixed training times there are limited places for individual online training sessions to accomodate to your schedule. We also have regular conditioning classes focused on the development of the endurance, strength and stability as the necessary foundation of your handbalancing practice.

If you want to join a class in russian language and receive more information such as pricing, please reach out to Olga on our main account. For english and german classes or individual training you can write Florian on our german account.
